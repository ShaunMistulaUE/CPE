What is Computer Engineering?

It is a four-year engineering program that embodies science and technology of design, development, implementation, maintenance, and integration of software and hardawre components in modern computing systems and computer controlled equipment.

Specializations

Software engineers focus on designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software applications and systems. They work on everything from mobile apps to operating systems and are skilled in programming languages and software development methodologies.

Embedded systems engineers design and program computer systems that are integrated into other devices, such as automotive control systems, medical devices, and IoT (Internet of Things) devices. They work on optimizing hardware and software to meet specific requirements.

Network engineers specialize in designing, configuring, and maintaining computer networks. They ensure that data can flow efficiently and securely between devices and across the internet. Network engineers may also work on cybersecurity aspects.

What Do Computer Engineering Students Do?

Computer Engineering Students deals with software and hardware related activities. They create software, build circuits, build systems, and for fun bits they challenge themeselves into creating their own system which consist of software and hardware. Their work can be quite diverse and may involve a wide range of tasks and responsibilities, depending on their specific roles and areas of expertise. Here are some common tasks and activities that computer engineers may engage in:

Arduino

Arduino is a popular open-source platform used by computer engineering students to learn about and experiment with embedded systems, electronics, and programming. It consists of both hardware and software components.

Logic Circuits

Logic circuits are fundamental components in computer engineering and digital electronics. They play a crucial role in processing and manipulating binary data (0s and 1s) within computers and electronic devices.

Software Design

Software design is a critical aspect of computer engineering, focusing on the process of creating well-structured, efficient, and maintainable software systems. For computer engineering students, understanding software design is essential.

About the Department of CpE

MISSION STATEMENT

The Department of Computer Engineering declares its role to produce competitive engineers who are knowledgeable in the fundamental areas of digital and computer system design, software development, and modeling of physical systems.
